About

Hey, thank you for visiting my website! I am a first year computer science student at Carleton University streaming in Computer and Internet Security. My interests are in cybersecurity, Networking and Cryptography, and Artificial Intelligence. I learned to code basic applications using visual basic by the time I was 10, but more recently learned Java, Arduino, and Python. I was introduced to the world of penetration testing a couple of years ago and quickly developed a passion for it. I began studying for my CCNA certification and completed online courses on Ethical Hacking Fundamentals and Vulnerability Assessment.
